Have You Seen Me    May 12, 2001 

 

An evening of queer spoken-word poetry performance & multidimensional art installation investigating the mysterious disappearance of queer SF artists in the ongoing eviction & gentrification crisis. Featured artists include: Ida Acton--kickin' ass for the workin' class; Lynn Breedlove & her orchestra--where Cole Porter meets punk rock; Julia Bloch--Treat Street hottie; Sonia Whittle--runs the Oakland Poetry slam, so shut the fuck up; Carla Richmond--new girl in town; Ariana Waynes--slammin' joyful to die for; and Sor Juana Inez de la Cruz--Renaissance erotic poetry babe. Also featuring art by Cooper Lee Bombardier.
