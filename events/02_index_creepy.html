SFinX: San Francisco in Exile
The Creepy Mommy Show
Saturday August 3, 2002 8:00pm

The show features honorary queer Daniel Handler (mistaken sometimes for Lemony Snicket) of the "Series of Unfortunate Events" books, Camille Roy "Cheap Speech" & "Swarm", actress Tina D'Elia "Groucho: A Day in the D'Elia Soup," poet Thea Hillman "Depending on the Light," Storm Florez "The Pussyboy Chronicles," and horehound stillpoint of "Out in the Castro" and many other collections before he heads off to represent San Francisco at the Gay Games in the 60 second poetry event, .

Featured performers will be reflecting on some of the bizarre circumstances that can arise from escaping from a mother, being mistaken for a mother, and actually becoming a mother, among other relevant hip topics to entertain an all-ages audience.
 

Tickets: $5-$10 sliding scale
Info/Reservations:
